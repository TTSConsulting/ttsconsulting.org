About TTS Consulting

TTS Consulting is a forward-thinking software development organization specializing in innovative solutions that bridge the gap between different computing environments. We focus on creating tools and applications that enhance developer productivity and provide seamless integration across platforms.

Our flagship project, WSBSD (Windows Subsystem for BSD), demonstrates our commitment to breaking down barriers between operating systems and providing developers with the tools they need to work efficiently across different environments.

With expertise in C#, web technologies, and system integration, we deliver robust solutions that solve real-world problems for developers and organizations worldwide.
